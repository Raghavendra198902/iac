# Analytics Dashboard User Guide

## Overview

The Analytics Dashboard provides comprehensive visualization and insights into your Enterprise Architecture responsibilities, metrics, and performance across all 15 responsibility areas.

**Access:** Navigate to `/ea/analytics-dashboard` or click "Analytics" in the EA menu.

---

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Key Metrics Overview

The top section displays 6 critical metrics cards:

#### Health Score
- **What it shows**: Overall EA health across all responsibility areas
- **Range**: 0-100%
- **Interpretation**:
  - 90-100%: Excellent - All areas performing optimally
  - 75-89%: Good - Most areas on track, minor improvements needed
  - 60-74%: Fair - Several areas need attention
  - Below 60%: Poor - Immediate action required

#### Active Initiatives
- **What it shows**: Number of currently active EA initiatives
- **Trend**: Shows increase/decrease compared to previous period
- **Action**: Click to view list of all active initiatives

#### Total Value
- **What it shows**: Cumulative value delivered by EA initiatives
- **Format**: USD currency
- **Calculation**: Sum of realized benefits from completed initiatives

#### Stakeholder Engagement
- **What it shows**: Number of active stakeholders across all areas
- **Trend**: Growth in stakeholder base
- **Importance**: Higher engagement typically indicates better EA adoption

#### Compliance Rate
- **What it shows**: Percentage of compliance with EA policies and standards
- **Target**: 95% or higher
- **Action**: Click to view compliance gaps

#### Total Documents
- **What it shows**: Count of all EA documentation
- **Types**: Strategies, policies, blueprints, standards, guidelines
- **Trend**: Documentation coverage growth

### 2. Responsibility Completion Chart

**Areas Displayed**:
1. Business Strategy Alignment
2. Technology Governance
3. Application Portfolio Management
4. Data Management
5. Security Architecture
6. Infrastructure Planning
7. Risk Management
8. Compliance Management
9. Change Management
10. Cost Management

**How to Use**:
- **Green bars** (80-100%): Areas performing well

- **Click on any bar** to drill down into that area's details

---

### 3. Monthly Trends Chart

**Type**: Line Chart

**Purpose**: Tracks 3 key metrics over time (last 6 months by default)

**Metrics Tracked**:
1. **Active Initiatives** (Blue line)
   - Shows number of initiatives launched each month
   - Helps identify activity patterns and resource planning needs

2. **Stakeholder Engagement** (Green line)
   - Tracks stakeholder participation over time
   - Indicates EA adoption and organizational buy-in

3. **Documentation Growth** (Purple line)
   - Monitors documentation coverage expansion
   - Ensures knowledge is being captured and shared

**How to Use**:
- **Hover** over data points to see exact values
- **Click legend items** to show/hide specific metrics
- **Zoom** by clicking and dragging on the chart
- **Export** chart data using the menu button (top-right)

**Interpretation**:
- **Upward trends**: Positive momentum, increasing EA maturity
- **Flat trends**: Stable state, may need revitalization
- **Downward trends**: Requires investigation and corrective action

---

### 4. Category Distribution Chart

**Type**: Pie Chart

**Purpose**: Shows distribution of initiatives across 5 major categories

**Categories**:
1. **Strategy** (Blue): Strategic planning and alignment initiatives
2. **Governance** (Green): Policy, compliance, and oversight
3. **Technology** (Purple): Technical architecture and infrastructure
4. **Data** (Orange): Data management and analytics
5. **Operations** (Red): Day-to-day operational improvements

**How to Use**:
- **Hover** over segments to see counts and percentages
- **Click** on segments to filter dashboard to that category
- Use this to **identify gaps** in initiative coverage

**Best Practice**: Aim for balanced distribution with no category below 15%

---

### 5. Maturity Assessment Radar

**Type**: Radar Chart

**Purpose**: Visualizes maturity levels across 6 key dimensions

**Dimensions**:
1. **Process**: Standardization and repeatability of EA processes
2. **Tools**: Adoption and effectiveness of EA tools
3. **Skills**: Team capabilities and competencies
4. **Culture**: Organizational EA awareness and support
5. **Governance**: Decision-making frameworks and oversight
6. **Delivery**: Ability to execute and deliver value

**Maturity Levels**:
- **Level 1 - Initial**: Ad-hoc, unpredictable processes
- **Level 2 - Managed**: Processes documented and repeatable
- **Level 3 - Defined**: Processes standardized across organization
- **Level 4 - Quantitatively Managed**: Processes measured and controlled
- **Level 5 - Optimizing**: Continuous improvement focus

**How to Use**:
- **Blue line** shows current maturity
- **Dashed line** shows target maturity
- **Gaps** between lines indicate improvement opportunities
- Click dimension labels for detailed assessment results

---

### 6. Recent Achievements

**Purpose**: Displays recent wins and milestones

**Information Shown**:
- Achievement title
- Related responsibility area
- Date accomplished
- Impact indicator (High/Medium/Low)

**How to Use**:
- Review regularly to stay informed of progress
- Share with stakeholders in reports and presentations
- Click "View All" to see complete achievement history

## Time Range Selector

Located at the top-right of the dashboard, this controls the time period for all metrics.

**Options**:
- **7 Days**: Week-over-week short-term view
- **30 Days**: Month-over-month standard view (default)
- **90 Days**: Quarterly trends and patterns
- **1 Year**: Annual performance and strategic planning

**When to Use Each**:
- **7 Days**: Daily operations monitoring, sprint reviews
- **30 Days**: Monthly reports, tactical planning
- **90 Days**: Quarterly business reviews, trend analysis
- **1 Year**: Annual planning, strategic roadmapping

1. **Set Time Range**: Select appropriate period

1. Click the **Share** button (top-right)
2. Options:
   - **Generate Link**: Create shareable URL with filters
   - **Email**: Send snapshot to stakeholders
   - **Schedule**: Set up automated email reports

### Drilling Down into Details

1. **Click any metric card** to see detailed view
2. **Click chart elements** to filter dashboard
3. **Use breadcrumbs** at top to navigate back

### Customizing View

1. Click **Customize** button
2. Options:
   - Rearrange chart positions (drag and drop)
   - Show/hide specific metrics
   - Change default time range
   - Set custom thresholds for alerts

## Troubleshooting

### Dashboard Not Loading
- **Check internet connection**
- **Refresh browser** (Ctrl+F5 / Cmd+Shift+R)
- **Clear browser cache**
- **Contact support** if issue persists

### Data Not Updating
- Check **last updated** timestamp
- Data refreshes every **5 minutes** automatically
- Click **Refresh** button to force update
- Verify you have proper **permissions**

### Charts Not Displaying
- **Enable JavaScript** in browser
- **Disable ad blockers** temporarily
- **Try different browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- **Contact IT support** if problem continues

### Export Failing
- Check **pop-up blocker** settings
- Ensure sufficient **disk space**
- Try **different export format**
- Reduce **time range** if dataset is too large

2. **Limit concurrent users**: Large organizations may experience slowdown

## FAQ

**Q: How often is data updated?**  
A: Dashboard data refreshes every 5 minutes automatically. You can manually refresh anytime.

**Q: Can I customize which charts are displayed?**  
A: Yes, click the "Customize" button to show/hide charts and rearrange layout.

**Q: How do I download chart data?**

**Q: Can I schedule automated reports?**  
A: Yes, click "Share" > "Schedule" to set up daily, weekly, or monthly email reports.

**Q: What permissions do I need?**  
A: Basic view access for all users. Export and share require "Contributor" role or higher.

**Q: Can I embed charts in other tools?**  
A: Yes, use the "Embed" option to get iframe code or API endpoints.

A: Standard retention is 2 years. Contact admin for extended retention.

A: Enterprise plan includes custom chart builder. Contact sales for upgrade.
